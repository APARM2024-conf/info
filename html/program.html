Program

Tentative Schedule

  • August 26:
    Registration and Welcome Reception - Attendees will be able to register for the conference and, if desired, attend a welcome reception.
  • August 27:
    Opening and Conference Session - APARM 2024 will officially open with welcoming remarks and a keynote address.
  • August 28:
    A day with a keynote address in the morning followed by conference sessions.
  • August 29:
    Another day beginning with a keynote address followed by conference sessions. The day will conclude with a banquet.
  • August 30:
    Conference sessions and excursion - The final day will include conference sessions in the morning followed by an excursion. Please note that the date and details of the excursion are still being finalized.
  • Please note that this schedule is subject to changes as we continue to refine our conference program.
  • All speakers are principally required to present in person at the conference venue.
