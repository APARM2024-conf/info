Publication

Conference Proceedings

The proceedings will be published as an e-book by CRC Press. The book will be published after the conference, and attendees will be emailed tokens for access to the e-book after the book is published. Please note that abstract papers will not be published in the proceedings. However, all full and abstract papers will be available online during the conference.

Conference Booklet

The conference booklet contains one-page abstracts for all full and abstract papers, as well as other conference information such as the program, etc. We will distribute both a paper and an e-booklet at the conference.
